**A1. Warranty — what exactly do you offer?**

This is the **single most valuable question in this document.**

Your competitors in Pretoria almost all advertise the same thing: *6 months or 10,000 km*. On

warranty after they rebuilt the motor."*

If that is genuinely what you offer, it beats every workshop we looked at, by a wide margin —

- How long, and how many kilometres?
- Does it cover parts, labour, or both?

- What is excluded?
- Is it given to the customer in writing?

team what applies to your vehicle."
